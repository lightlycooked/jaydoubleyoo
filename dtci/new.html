WEEKLY SUMMARY
 
  • ABC News had the biggest positive week over week gains with 32.4M unique visitors ↑70% and 8.6M streamers ↑53%. The increase in minutes was due to the continued interest in the coverage of the protests, memorial and special guestsregarding George Floyd.
  • ESPN and ABC had the biggest declines year over year across all major KPI’s : unique visitors, streamers, and video minutes were ↓50%, ↓74%, and ↓63% for ESPN respectively and ↓46%, ↓46%, and ↓50% for ABC. The same week last year, both networks had the 'NBA Finals' which is typically a big draw for both networks and the biggest reason for the year-over-year decline.
